What is really software development?

2017-07-06 · 591 words · 3 minute read
general
software development

Do we really know it? At first hand, the question in title of this post could look like as a sily one, after all, this is a very well defined thing, right? Yeah, my question could be somehow sily, but I am inclined to believe it really touching in something. During all my years as a developer I was presented to many kinds of frameworks, methodologies, paradigms and strategies to develop software, as TDD, BDD, Agile, RAD, DDD, XP and many others, all of them being, in some sense, promoted as a better way or tool to make software.
